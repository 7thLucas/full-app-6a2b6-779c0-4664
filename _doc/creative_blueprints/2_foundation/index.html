The Foundation

Caught late costs deals.

The Problem

Teams get blindsided.

A competitor quietly cuts prices or ships a feature — and you only find out when a prospect drops it into a sales call. Today, watching rivals is manual and sporadic, living in browser tabs and memory. So changes are caught late, or missed entirely.

The Solution

One feed. Signal over noise.

PulseWatch tracks every competitor across pricing, product, and marketing — then surfaces only the handful of changes that matter, with alerts. Replace scattered manual checks with a single calm pane of glass, early enough to react.
